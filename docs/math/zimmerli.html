CELEBRATING CHINESE CALLIGRAPHY
AND CULTURAL ARTS

Wednesday, April 2 / 6pm
Tour and Calligraphy Demonstration

Yi-Zhi Huang

Join us for a fun and educational evening that features an artist's calligraphy demonstration by Yi-Zhi Huang complementing the exhibition Maples in the Mist: Chinese Poems for Children Illustrated by Jean and Mou-sien Tseng. The program begins with a tour of the exhibition by curator Marilyn Symmes, Director of the Morse Research Center for Graphic Arts at the museum, and a reading of selected poems from the book in their native language by Dietrich Tschanz. For program details, visit the webpage.
