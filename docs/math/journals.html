  • Communications in Contemporary Mathematics (CCM)

    Authors should submit their papers by email (PDF format) to a member of the Editorial Board. In exceptional circumstances, papers can also be sent by email (pdf format) to Haim Brezis for Analysis (including PDEs, differential geometry involving PDEs, and applied analysis), and to me for all other fields. When you send a paper to me for publication in Communications in Contemporary Mathematics, please indicate clearly in the subject line that this is a submission to CCM.
